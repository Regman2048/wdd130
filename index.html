Zimbabwe

Zimbabwe is a landlocked country in southern Africa known for its dramatic landscapes and diverse wildlife. Victoria Falls, one of the world's largest waterfalls, is a major tourist attraction on the border with Zambia. The country's rich history includes the ancient city of Great Zimbabwe, and its people are known for their resilience and vibrant culture.
